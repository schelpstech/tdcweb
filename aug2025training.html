August 2025 Educators’ Training

Theme: THE EVOLVING EDUCATOR

Date: 14th – 15th August 2025
Time: 8:00 AM – 3:30 PM daily
Venue: Redeemers High School (RHS), Redemption City

Join us for a transformative three-day professional learning event hosted by the Teachers Development Centre (TDC). Designed for educators and school leaders across CRSM schools and beyond, this training is a unique opportunity to learn, connect, and lead with purpose.

Through interactive sessions and expert-led discussions, participants will explore cutting-edge strategies to enhance student outcomes, promote teacher well-being, and drive innovation within today’s dynamic education system.

“Shaping the future of education requires vision, passion, and empowered leadership.”
Training Logistics

Participants will enjoy well-organized logistics including affordable accommodation options, flexible training fees (with or without meals), and access to all necessary materials such as certificates, name tags, charts, and whiteboards. The venue will be fully equipped to ensure a productive and comfortable experience throughout the event.

We warmly invite all CRSM educators and stakeholders to be part of this empowering experience. Let us come together to envision new possibilities, empower one another, and excel as we shape the future of our schools—together.
